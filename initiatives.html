Our Green Economy initiatives

The radical green economy initiatives deals with systems of economic activities related to the production, distribution and consumption of goods and services that results in improved human wellbeing over long term while not exposing future generation to significant environmental risks or ecological scarcities. Tlhabollo ya tikoloho.

With the aim and objective to reduce the amount of waste that end up in our landfills. Develop a green industrial buyback centre, turning waste into to use/products or energy for example paving bricks, roofing tiles, etc.

The mission of our initiatives include
1 Coordinate Mass Cleanup Campaigns
2 Declare War on leaks (Saving Water Campaign)
3 Horticulture (Tree Planting, Schools & home back yard gardens)
4 Host Free State Green EXPO, Green School’s Competition, and Green Schools Awards
5 Green Jobs “Introduce recycle and earn” - train people, on fulltime and part time on green industrial initiatives
6 Environmental Education Campaigns: Educate Community and Schools about climate change, mitigation and adaptation methods
